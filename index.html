She lives where
everything is soft

Vaireya's mornings start slow. There's always music — something that sits somewhere between Hindi film and bedroom indie. She photographs the light through her curtains before she does anything else. Her world has a city apartment, a forest home, a villa. She moves between them the way some people move between moods.

The things she recommends are things she lives with. The candle on her shelf. The coat she wore on a Tuesday with nowhere to be. The serum that made her skin feel like itself again. Nothing is for the camera. Everything just happens to be beautiful.

This Morning City Apartment
The kitchen at 7am smells like croissants and everything feels possible
I put on my silk pyjamas and stood by the window for a long time. The city was already loud but my kitchen was still soft. I made coffee, put on Prateek Kuhad, and just... stayed there. On my counter — the serum I've been using for three weeks now. My skin has never felt more like mine. I'll link it below for anyone who asks.
